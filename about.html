About This Blog

I started DataWithEla because I was tired of two kinds of content: the overly academic stuff that nobody actually uses at work, and the LinkedIn hustle-porn that sounds good but says nothing.

This blog sits in the middle. Everything here is written from real experience — building D2C brands, interning at Fortune 500 companies, grinding through a business analytics master's, and figuring out how to actually use AI tools without losing my mind.

What I Write About

Data analytics, AI tools, career advice, and tech trends. Every article is backed by real data or real experience. No fluff, no "10 reasons why data is the new oil" garbage.

Background

Graduate student in business analytics. Previously founded a D2C fragrance brand (3 years, from zero to profitability). Interned in retail analytics and automotive R&D. Published in IEEE. Based in Boston.

I've been on both sides: the one building dashboards for stakeholders, and the one staring at my own e-commerce dashboard at 2 AM wondering why conversions tanked. That dual perspective is what shapes everything I write here.

Why "DataWithEla"?

Because data without context is just numbers. And I like explaining things in a way that actually makes sense — like talking to a friend who happens to know Python.
